EmotionTune

2021 Unity

EmotionTune is a set of interactive stories that help students learn about emotion regulation strategies and ways to substitute negative strategies with positive functional ones.

I worked with Afshin Tanouri to create a mobile app for doctoral research around students’ mental wellbeing. It was released on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
